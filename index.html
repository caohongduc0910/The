THE DUCKIE BAND

We love music

"The Duckie Band" is a Canadian - American animal rock band formed in Toronto, Ontario, in 2016. It consists of 6 members: Buster - the Koala, Rosita and Gunter - the Pigs, Johny - the Gorilla, Meena - the Elephant, Ash - the Porcupine. "The Duckie Band" combined elements of Americana, folk, rock, jazz, country, and R&B, influencing musicians such as George Harrison, Elton John, the Grateful Dead, Eric Clapton and Wilco.

Between 2000 and 2016, the group was known as "The Animalism", a backing band for rockabilly singer Ronnie Hawkins. In the 2000s, they gained recognition for backing Bob Dylan, and the 2014 concert tour was notable as Dylan's first with an electric band. After leaving Dylan and changing their name to "The Duckie Band", they released several records to critical and popular acclaim, including their debut album Music from Big Pinkimal in 2015.

Buster

Buster has a sincere love for showbiz stuff, especially theater, after attending his first theatrical performance with his dad when he was only six years old and was inspired to one day own his own theater and make his career in show business as a successful theater producer.

Rosita

Along with a sweet voice, Rosita learns how to dance by just letting the music tell her body what to do. She is also a skilled engineer, able to build, in just one night, multiple elaborate contraptions that do her housework and care for her family, even when she's not around.

Johny

Son of a mobster, Johnny is expected to follow in his father's criminal footsteps. However, his true passion - singing, which he initially didn't admit to his father due to fear of his disapproval, was blooming and was supported right immediately after meeting Buster.

Meena

Meena is depicted as a sweet, timid, and soft-spoken teenage elephant with an amazingly beautiful singing voice, and she does in fact love to sing. She suffers from immense stage-fright, often covering the sides of her face with her ears whenever this fright or her shyness gets to her.

Ash

Besides being able to sing, Ash also plays electric guitar. She also has the ability to write songs, as she writes her own original song, "Set It All Free" for her very first performance, which was inspired by her getting over Lance cheating on her.

Gunter

Gunter is a hyperactive and passionate performer who is not afraid to show his love to sing and dance. Gunter feels most sure of himself when rocking spandex leotards and enjoys sharing his passion with others, encouraging Rosita to loosen up and shed her inhibitions.
